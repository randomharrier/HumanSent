Mission

We believe instant communication trained us to be casual with our words. HumanSent is a small rebellion: thoughtful messages, written by hand, mailed across the real world, and received like something that matters.

HumanSent is for: birthdays, apologies, gratitude, hard conversations, love notes, and anything you’d rather not “fire-and-forget.”

HumanSent is not for: real-time chat, edits, or infinite takes.

HumanSent Product Laws

These aren’t “features.” They’re the point.

  1. No instant delivery. Every message must be physically mailed.
  2. All handwriting is real. No fonts, no robots, no plotters.
  3. Photos must be taken live in-app. Zero preview. Not from camera roll.
  4. Messages cannot be edited once sent. No undo. No recall.
  5. No digital copies stored after writing. If it exists, it exists as a physical card only.
